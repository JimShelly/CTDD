CTDD - Your Partner in Cancer Research

 

 

This office has provided full statistical and data management support for over 450 GOG trials and over 110,000 patient registrations.

 

 

Enrolled over 115,000 patients, 46,000 using full suite of proprietary software systems, captured over 2.4 million eCRFs, generated over 350,000 queries (2/3 automated), tracked over 250,000 specimens, facilitated over 17,000 electronic case reviews.

 

 

These trials include 119 Phase 3 clinical trials, 4 Phase 2/3 clinical trials, over 50 phase 1, and over 290 phase 2 trials, most of which were government funded.

Several FDA indications for Paclitaxel, Bevacizumab, and Topotecan for Ovarian and/or Cervix cancer were based on GOG trials.
